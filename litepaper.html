MOJI CLUB LITEPAPER

Moji Club is the first NFT project to allow complete token personnalization, thanks to a unique custom mint system.

Create your very own Avatar using our configurator, from hundreds of customization traits, and get the certification that your Avatar belongs to you and you alone thanks to the Ethereum blockchain.

A COMPLETELY NEW TECH

All NFTs projects you know are based on a Mint → Reveal system.

Moji Club works differently, generating each NFT directly during the Mint process. Since the blockchain data is public, the traits scarcity is managed off-chain in a remote server containing an Ethereum private key.

If the avatar traits are available, the server uploads the NFT on the IPFS and returns a cryptographic proof that is verified on-chain. This prevents cheaters from consciously selecting the rarest traits.

HOW DOES IT WORKS ?

The unicity of each Moji is guaranteed by the Ethereum blockchain. Once your Moji is set up and minted, a representation of its traits is permanently stored in the blockchain.
