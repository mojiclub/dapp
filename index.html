THE SPECS

Each Moji Club PFP is unique and configurable from 150+ traits including hair, clothes, accessories and more. All Moji Club PFPs are dope, but some are rarer than others.

The PFPs are stored as ERC-721 tokens on the Ethereum blockchain, and their characteristics are stored on IPFS. Purchasing a Generation 0 PFP costs 0.25 ETH.

Minting a Generation 1 requires owning at least one Moji Club Ticket. One ticket will be burned in the PFP minting process.
