FAIR MINTING

To make sure no funny business goes on during the minting period, a particular emphasis has been placed on safety and security regarding the PFPs traits scarcity.


Because the Ethereum blockchain is completely public, storing the traits scarcity on-chain was not an option. To solve this problem, we used a centralized server that checks itself if a PFP can be minted, without revealing the traits scarcity.

Checking a PFP availability

1 A base36 hash is generated from all traits selected.

2 The hash is sent to a centralized server to check the availability of all the selected traits.

3 If all traits are available, the server replies back with a ECDSA signature of the hash. Else, it replies with the list of sold out traits and disables the said traits from the Configuratooor.

4 If your PFP is available, the Mint button will become available, and your wallet will prompt you to sign the transaction.

5 The signature returned by the server is verified on-chain during the mint transaction.
